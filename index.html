All entries are anonymous, with no information about you or your machined saved. Be free and express yourself. Any entries that threaten the ability for others to feel safe and free to share will be removed.

You (collectively) allows visitors to reflect on how they define themselves, as a group. The site allows you to learn, empathize and connect with others also on a self-discovery journey.
